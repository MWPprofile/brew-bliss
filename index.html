About Us

Founded in the heart of Baguio in 2020, Brew Bliss began as a small dream shared by three friends who believed in the power of coffee to bring people together. From our first pour-over to our locally sourced beans, our passion lies in creating an experience—not just a beverage. We take pride in our ethically sourced coffee, warm ambiance, and commitment to our community. Whether you're grabbing a quick espresso or staying for a cozy afternoon, we want you to feel at home.
